INGREDIENTS

For Raspberry Sauce:
- 2 Packages (6 ounces or 1 1/3 cup each) Driscoll's Raspberries
- 1/4 Cup granulated sugar


For Crust:
- 1/4 Cup granulated sugar
- 1 1/2 Teaspoons cornstarch
- 1/2 Cup pecans, toasted
- 1/3 Cup granulated sugar
- 5 Tablespoons butter, melted
- 10 whole chocolate graham crackers, broken into pieces


For Cheesecake
- 4 Packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, at room temperature
- 2/3 Cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 Cup sour cream
- 1 Tbsp. cornstarch
- 1/3 Cup Grand Marnier or orange flavored liqueur
- 1 Tsp. vanilla extract
- 4 large eggs 


For Raspberry Cheesecake Topping
- 6 ounces or 1 1/3 cup of Raspberry compote or fresh frozen raspberries
- 2 Tablespoons confectioners' sugar
